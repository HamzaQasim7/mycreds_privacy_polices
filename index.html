Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026

1. Data Collection

MyCreds collects minimal data necessary for app functionality. We do not collect personal information, browsing history, or track your usage patterns. All password data is stored locally on your device and encrypted using AES-256-GCM encryption.

2. Data Storage

Your passwords are encrypted and stored locally on your device. If you enable cloud sync, encrypted data is stored on secure servers. We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256-GCM) to protect your data. Your master passcode is never stored or transmitted.

3. Zero-Knowledge Architecture

MyCreds uses a zero-knowledge architecture. This means we cannot access, view, or recover your passwords. Only you have access to your master passcode, which is required to decrypt your data.

4. Cloud Sync

Cloud sync is optional. If enabled, your encrypted data is synced across your devices using secure servers. The encryption key is derived from your master passcode and never leaves your device.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for cloud storage and sync services. All data transmitted to third-party services is encrypted. We do not share your data with advertisers or analytics services.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your data at any time. You can export your passwords or delete your account. All data is deleted when you uninstall the app.

7. Security Measures

We implement multiple security layers including encryption, secure memory handling, and automatic data clearing when the app goes to background. We regularly update security measures to protect your data.

8. Children's Privacy

MyCreds is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app settings or support channels.
